## 📊 Practice Sites for Data Analysis and Visualization

> A curated list of websites and platforms to learn and practice data analysis, data visualization, and related skills.  
> These resources are ideal for building a strong foundation in data manipulation, statistical analysis, and creating insightful visualizations.  
> Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your skills, these platforms have something for everyone.

***Still being updated!!!***

___

### [DataWars](https://www.datawars.io/)

*DataWars* is a quiet little corner where you can grow your data analysis skills through real-world challenges.  
Whether it’s Python, R, or SQL, every exercise feels like planting a small seed — slowly but surely building your abilities, one step at a time.

### [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/)

*Kaggle* is like a wide open field for data dreamers.  
You can wander through countless datasets, join competitions, or simply learn by exploring projects from brilliant minds around the world.  
It’s a perfect place to experiment, grow, and let your curiosity lead the way.

### [StrataScratch](https://www.stratascratch.com/)

*StrataScratch* offers real interview questions from top tech companies like Facebook, Amazon, and Google.  
It's a gentle but focused way to practice SQL and Python skills, helping you prepare for real-world challenges while building confidence along the journey.
